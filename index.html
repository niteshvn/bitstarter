Typical Saturday afternoon out with friends and shopping loved this red dress and ready to make the purchase but not sure if you recently purchaed a similar looking dress sitting at the drycleaner or if you already have all the accessories that you need that would go along or you don't know what I have in my closet for that have no clue I bought them or how they look anymore. So typically we end up buying a similar looking stuff and after a month or two realize we have a similar one already..oops too late to return! Sounds similar well that is the problem we are trying to solve here. The best thing, it will help you stay organized and at the same time you have a pretty good idea of what all accessories you have, the size, time when you purchased them so let's say if there is a clothes or coats drive in your community for charity you know for sure what you have out with you for a long time and may be can be given for a charity. All so much easily manageable.

Even thought the problem sounds simple but it's complex if you think about it but we want to keep the complexity hidden for you and bring the final product to the users to make your life easy with one of the most important aspect of your life which is how you present yourself to others and which has direct correlation to what you buy. So the app/website should help you organize what you have in your fashion vault in the most virtual and exciting way that you are always on the go and never wasting any money in buying stuff that you don't need or already have or never miss out on that piece of jewelry that would add the final touch to your fashion to make you look fabulous.
