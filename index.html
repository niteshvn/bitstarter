We are looking to design a smart phone app for on the go jewelry design app which is both android and iOS compatible and let's people capture their creativity on the go and also share the final design with anyone they like. Currently this type of free hand app does not exist and we believe there is a potential to it specially in the fast growing economies like India and China where more and more people buy precious custom made jewelry of their choice but have limitations in terms of what design they can have. This app does not mean a substitute to high end jewelry design software that is currently available but is a convenient addition to their world to explore and enjoy their creativity.

With this app, you can custom design your own jewelry whenever you want wherever you want on your smartphone. The end goal is to integrate this with the jewelry design machines to be able to mass produce replica or real jewelry inspired by your design on the smartphone. The real challenge is to be able to bring all different shapes, colors, various jewelry stones, precious and semi precious stones, precious metal and make the end product look seamless on your smartphone that you feel proud of your design and feel like sharing with your friends on your social profile and get their feedback. Depending on that you either modify the design or you use it as is to actually get a replica/jewelry created out of it. The end goal it to make the start to end process really seamless and fun and at the end deliver something creative to you which you can feel proud of wearing per your fashion needs whenever you like.
